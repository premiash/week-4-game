Crystal Game

Instructions
You will be given a random target score at the start of the game. Click the four crystals below which will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Match your total score to random target score to win, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random target score.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
